Welcome to the parish web site for St James' Woodside

Do you want to know more about St James? Then take a look at our aims. Below are details of some of our upcoming events.

Church Camp 2005
Dust off those tents, it’s camping time again! The dates for this year Church Camp are 17th to 19th June. This year we are travelling further afield to the East Coast, and are looking at campsites in the Scarborough/Filey area. If you are interested in coming along could you please fill in the Camping Sheet at the back of church? If you would like to know more, then please have a word with either John and Karen Evans or Chris and Helen Davis. Once we have a rough idea of numbers, we can then go ahead and book. An itinerary will be issued nearer the date.

Home Entertainment Sale
“Home Entertainment Sale” (videos, DVDs, CDs and tapes) on the morning of October 15th in the Parish Centre, in aid of Church funds.
